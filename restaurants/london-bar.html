London Bar

Located: Nou de la Rambla 34, 080001 BarcelonaSpain

A legendary bar which opened in 1910. It was frequented by notable people such as Ernest Hemingway, Pablo Picasso, Joan Miró or Salvador Dalí. Situated in Carrer Nou de la Rambla, 34.The difference now is that the tourists know about this bar. And barcelona is now flooded with tourists. But that doesn't matter... I still managed to meet locals in this bar to practice spanish with. Always a friendly crowd. This bar has a history. And you can see it in the photographs hanging on the wall. The decor is kinda funky which I love. And a great place to have a beer and meet friendly people.
