The Tickets restaurant is the new concept made by Ferran Adria (the chef of El Bulli, for many years the best restaurant of the world) and his brother Albert, together with the Iglesias brothers, owners of the Rias de Galicia, one of the best sea food restaurants in Barcelona.

The facility consists of several bars magically decorated and themed. While you eat, it appears like you are participants of a movie, a theater or a vaudeville circus, a really different concept.

It is very difficult to book a table, every day at 0h they open the tables for 2 months ahead, please check the detail instructions for the booking online at their website: www.ticketsbar.es/en/reservas
