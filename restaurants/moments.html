Restaurant Moments

Located: Passeig de Gràcia, 38, 08007 Barcelona, Spain.

Moments is located inside Barcelona’s Mandarin Oriental hotel and has an understated and elegantly gilded decor. A small, but appreciated touch on each table is a small, dramatically lit flower. Service is warm, friendly, efficient and multilingual – at least Portuguese and English are spoken alongside Catalan and Castilian.

This is a 2 Star Michelin Restaurant in the Mandarin oriental Hotel. I emailed to make a reservation for two people. I have traveled to Europe extensively and have been to many Michelin starred restaurants. This restaurant wanted a deposit of Euro125 per person to secure a table. I was shocked that the deposit went "non -refundable" 24 hours before the actual dinner event.
